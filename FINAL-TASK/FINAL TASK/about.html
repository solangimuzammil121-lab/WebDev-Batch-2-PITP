Our Story

The Taste Haven Story

Founded in 2010 by Chef Marco Bellini, Taste Haven began as a small family restaurant with a big dream: to bring authentic, flavorful cuisine to our community. What started as a 30-seat eatery has grown into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: use the freshest local ingredients, combined with traditional cooking techniques and modern creativity. Every dish tells a story, and every meal is crafted with care and attention to detail.

Today, we continue to honor our roots while innovating and evolving. We believe that great food brings people together, and we're proud to be part of your special moments.

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Marco Bellini

Executive Chef & Owner

With over 25 years of culinary experience, Chef Marco brings passion and expertise to every dish. Trained in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, he returned to his hometown to create Taste Haven.

"For me, cooking is about creating memories. I want every guest to leave with a story to tell and a desire to return."
